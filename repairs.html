Bumper March 2021
When I first bought the car, it had red tape on the fender and front bumper to hold the front bumper onto the car. This is because the previous owner hit something and cracked it. My dad taught me how to fibreglass and then we resprayed it, including the underneath. We then fixed the fitment and it was perfect. The next night I scratched it.

Bearing October 2021
After 7 months of driving the car I begin to hear a noise from the rear wheels when I drove. I first replaced the wheel bearings in both the rear suspension, which did not fix the issue. Later on I ordered two new wheel hubs and two more bearings as you had to 'break' the bearings to replace the hubs. This fixed the issue.
Rust January 2022
While I had covid, I wanted to work on MR2. So I found the only point of (visible) rust on the car and fixed it. I sat in the frunk and sanded the frunk underneath (bonnet lid) and the surrounding area of the frunk. This was mostly surface rust, so I got rid of all of it, sprayed rust preventer, undercoat and used and aircompressor to respray the red, with the help of my dad. The colour match to the fender is perfect. I did not do the entire frunk because effort.

Rear March 2021
When I first bought the car, the black trim in the rear was really faded. I wanted to permantly fix it so I sanded down the metal diffuser/trim piece and resprayed black. Made the rear look much better. Also resprayed the wipers and then got fined by a HWP because he pulled me over.

Aircon February 2021
The old owner stated that the car's aircon only needed a re-gas to be fixed. So I got it re-gassed, but it stopped working almost straight away. I then replaced all of the seals for the aircon connections that span from the front to the rear of the car. It stilled leaked after another re-gas. I then got my aircon compressor refurbished and the aircon was fixed
Bumper pt 2 March 2022
During the bad floods of early 2022, my car's targa top continued to leak no matter what I did. I decided to take it to my grandmas garage, but on the way in I hit a piece of wood that was on the ground. It cracked my bumper that I fixed the year before. Havent fixed it yet
Clean January 2021
The paint on the car was very faded and swirled. Due to the fact that it is 30 year old paint and one stage paint (made w/o clear-coat), I couldn't fully restore the paint to perfection. Despite this I clay barred, compounded, polished and ceramic coated the entire car which took about 2 days of work.

Engine 1 March 2021
My car always found it hard to turn on after it had rained or the engine bay had gotten wet. One day when I was driving it, I could tell that it was running on 3 cylinders (I believe). I then found out that there was a crack in my distributor cap. So I used the distributor cap off our spare engine, and also bought a new distributor and spark plugs. This fixed the issue.
Engine bay January 2022
I always hated that my engine bay grill was a very faded red, with peeling clear coat. There was absolutely no way to take the layer apart as they are factory welded on n/a mr2's. So I taped off every single grill piece and used a tiny paint gun to spray inside. It isn't the most perfect finish as I couldn't sand the inside or polish it afterwards. Stills looks better than previously
Handbrake December 2021
When I was replacing the wheel bearings, I noticed my handbrake cable was completely snapped. I always wondered why my handbrake never worked. So I bought a new cable for it and replaced it.
Wheel Studs June 2021
I always noticed that my wheel studs were a bit rusty, but I wasn't too worried as my wheel nuts were open holed. Then one day I was taking my wheel off and one snapped. I decided I would replace them and ordered them from Toyota. Another snapped off before I replaced them. Now I have 20 fresh studs
Engine Strut September 2021
The strut bar in my engine bar had lots of rust so I decided to sand it and respray it black. I forgot to take an after pic
Cleaning wheels 2021
The old owner of my car was an old man and the car was very dirty. I decided to deep clean my Enkei wheels as the barrel was so dirty. Came out very clean.
Window January 2021
When I bought the car the window regulator had just failed and in the week before I picked the car up, the old owner put a new regulator in. He did not line the drivers side window up at all and there was a massive gap. So my dad helped me line up both windows as they both had massive gaps and were diagonal.
